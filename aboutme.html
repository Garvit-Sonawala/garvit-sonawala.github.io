Student Intern - IIT Guwahati

July 2024 – September 2024

Under the guidance of Dr. Subhaditya Bhattacharya, In this work, I studied the evolution of particle number densities in the expanding universe using the Boltzmann equation. By analyzing how expansion and interaction rates affect the comoving yield of particles, I derived the key relation governing freeze-out behavior during the radiation-dominated epoch. The study highlights how particle annihilation and creation processes shape relic abundances and extends naturally to exploring non-standard cosmological scenarios and dark-matter models beyond the Standard Model.
